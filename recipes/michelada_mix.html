Michelada Mix Recipe


Description

This is the Michelada Mix Recipe from Taco Joint.


Ingredients

  • 32oz bloody mary mix
  • 4oz oranje juice
  • 2oz lime juice
  • 3oz valentina black label salsa
  • 2 bunches cilantro diced
  • 1oz black pepper

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a large container
  2. Mix thouroughly
  3. Add 3oz to your favorite mexican beer over ice!
